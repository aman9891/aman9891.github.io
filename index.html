Lessons learnt from Trekking ?

I have always loved exploring different treks and it has become my habit to complete atleast on big trek every year. The reason is not only enjoying and having a break but also new experiences and learnings on every trek. It is about changing your perspective, giving-up leisurely life and comforts, living in camps with no mobile connectivity, helping and taking care of each other at every step, spending nights in the cold, cleaning trails and many many more things. It's a continous learning process and spending time together.
